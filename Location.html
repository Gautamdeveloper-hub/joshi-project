About
Palanchok Bhagwati Temple is a Hindu place of worship located in Panchkhal Municipality, approximately 42 km from Kathmandu. The temple features a 3 feet tall idol of the Goddess Bhagavati carved out of black stone. This temple is believed to have been constructed during the reign of King Mānadeva.

Address: Palanchok - Bhagawati Rd, Panchkhal 45212
Hours: Open ⋅ Closes 5:30 PM
Deity: Devi
District: Kavrepalanchok
